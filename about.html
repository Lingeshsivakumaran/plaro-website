About PLARO

We built the platform we wished existed

An innovative career ecosystem built by students who felt the gap between what college teaches and what jobs demand — and decided to close it.

🎯

Our Mission

Make every student industry-ready

To give every student in India — regardless of college brand or city — access to the skills, simulations, and connections they need to build a real career. We help companies find verified, capable talent, and we help students become that talent.

💡

Our Vision

A world where your skills matter more than your college name

To become India's leading platform for the combination of education and social media — where a student from a tier-3 college can prove their ability, land opportunities, and build a career that matches their potential, not just their pedigree.

Why We Built PLARO

It started with a frustrating observation: students were spending hours scrolling on social media, but 4 years in college and still graduating unprepared. The systems we use every day to connect are addictive but unproductive, while the systems we use to learn are productive but unengaging.

We saw classmates with genuine talent struggling to get their first job because education alone wasn't enough. They lacked the right experience, the right exposure, and a modern way to prove what they could do beyond a marksheet. At the same time, the social platforms where they spent their time offered no real career value.

PLARO is our answer: the ultimate combination of education and social media. We brought the engaging, community-driven mechanics of social platforms into the world of career development.

Our mission is simple but profound: we are turning scrollers into learners. By offering AI personalization, real-world simulations, verifiable skill portfolios, and genuine opportunities all in one place, we are making career readiness as intuitive and engaging as your favorite social feed.

Business Model

How PLARO grows

Three revenue streams, two sides of the market, one ecosystem.

Students
🎓
Freemium Student Tier
Core features free for all students. Premium tier unlocks advanced Taikens, enhanced portfolio tools, and priority opportunity matching.
Revenue: Subscriptions + In-app upgrades
Companies
🏢
B2B Talent Access
Companies pay to access PLARO's verified talent pool, post opportunities, and search candidates by domain, rank, and simulation scores.
Revenue: B2B SaaS subscriptions
Marketplace
💸
Freelance Revenue Share
PLARO takes a platform fee on all freelance and gig transactions completed through the platform's opportunity matching system.
Revenue: Transaction commission
